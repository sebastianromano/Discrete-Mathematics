==Mean==
- the ==average== of the numbers
In other words, it is the ==sum== ==divided== by the ==count==

Calculate the ==Mean== 
| description                               | math                     |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| add up all the numbers                    | 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 = ==15== |
| then divide by how many numbers there are | 15 / 5 = ==3==             |
